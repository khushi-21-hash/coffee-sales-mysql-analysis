# Coffee Sales Data Analysis using MySQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes coffee sales data across multiple Indian cities using MySQL. The objective is to derive business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and market opportunities.

## Dataset

The project uses four tables:

- City
- Customers
- Products
- Sales

## SQL Concepts Used

- Joins
- Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
- Aggregate Functions
- Window Functions
- DENSE_RANK()
- LAG()
- Date Functions

## Business Questions Solved

1. Coffee Consumers Count
2. Total Revenue Analysis
3. Product Sales Analysis
4. Average Sales per Customer
5. City Population vs Coffee Consumers
6. Top Selling Products by City
7. Customer Segmentation
8. Average Sale vs Rent Analysis
9. Monthly Sales Growth
10. Market Potential Analysis

## Key Findings

- Pune generated the highest revenue.
- Delhi showed the largest potential coffee consumer base.
- Jaipur demonstrated strong customer acquisition with relatively low rent.
- Monthly sales growth varied significantly across cities.
